Koshary




koshari is Egypt's national dish and a widely popular street food. It is a traditional Egyptian staple, mixing pasta, Egyptian fried rice, vermicelli and brown lentils,and other ingridients






Macarona Bechamel




Macarona Bechamel is essentially Egypt's take on a Bechamel pasta bake. Typically, it's made from layers of penne pasta, spiced meat and tomato sauce, and bechamel.
