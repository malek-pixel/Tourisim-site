Abdelhalim Hafez




Abdel Halim Hafez, was an Egyptian singer, actor, conductor, businessman, music teacher and film producer. He is widely regarded as a pivotal figure in 20th-century Arabic popular music.






Umm Kulthum




Umm Kulthum was an Egyptian singer songwriter and film actress active from the 1920s to the 1970s. She was given the honorific title Kawkab el-Sharq. Immensely known throughout the Arab World.






Amir eid




Amir Eid is a prominent Egyptian singer, songwriter, and actor, best known as the lead vocalist of the rock band Cairokee.
